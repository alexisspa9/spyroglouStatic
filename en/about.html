ABOUT US





Spyrogloy HLME specializes in the design, construction, and implementation of electrical installations for both private and public projects, while also providing professional electrical engineering services.

Since 1991, we have been active in the field of electrical and electromechanical engineering, building extensive experience and expertise across a wide range of projects. Our core areas of specialization include:

  • Design, supervision, and construction of electrical installations.
  • Interior and exterior lighting design and installation.
  • Lightning protection systems.
  • Building automation systems.
  • Design, programming, and installation of KNX systems.

Driven by quality, consistency, and reliability, we undertake electrical projects of every scale, delivering comprehensive solutions that meet the highest standards. Professionalism, technical expertise, and the use of certified materials are the core values that have established us as a trusted partner in the electrical engineering industry.

Selected Projects

  • Supervisor of overhead power distribution networks for the Public Power Corporation (PPC) in the Athens region.
  • 1991–2022: Electrical installations for private and public projects on the island of Paros.
  • 1994–2004: OTE subcontractor throughout the Cyclades Islands through Paros Technical Company O.E.
  • 2002–2019: Maintenance and repair of the municipal street lighting network for the Municipality of Paros.
  • 2007–2010: PPC Paros contractor for electrical service connections.
  • 2014–2019: Maintenance and repair of the municipal street lighting network for the Municipality of Antiparos.
  • 2018–2020: PPC Paros contractor for electrical service connections.
  • 2022–Present: Establishment of Spyrogloy HLME, continuing our long-standing activity in electrical engineering studies, electrical installations, building automation, KNX systems, and electrical engineering services for private and public sector projects.
