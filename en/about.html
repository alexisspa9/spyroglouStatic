ABOUT US





Our company Spyroglou Stelios covers the provision of services for the design and implementation of electrical installations for private and public works as well as electrical engineering services. We have been presenting a long activity in the field of electromechanical studies and installations since 1991 as freelancers with main activity the study and supervision of electrical installations, lighting design, automation, outdoor lighting design and lightning protection. Having as background many years of experience, we undertake electrical projects of all types with high quality services. The consistency of every kind of work, the credibility with regard to the materials we use, the seriousness and the responsibility towards our work and, of course, towards our business clients made us what we are today. 


Below we present examples of works that we have been working on:

  • Foreman Network Operator at PPC S.A. Hellas in Athens area.
  • 1994-2004: OTE subcontracting in Cyclades, with our company Paros Technical Company OE.
  • 2002-2019: the repair of faults in the Lamps of the Municipality of Paros.
  • 2007-2010: Paros PPC's contract for electrical supplies.
  • 2018-2020: Paros PPC's contract for electrical supplies.
  • 2014-2019: the repair of faults in the Lamps of the Municipality of Antiparos.
  • 1991 until today: electrician in the island of Paros for private and public works.
