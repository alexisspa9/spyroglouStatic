About us






SPYROGLOU HLME



Spyroglou HLME provides study, design, construction, and implementation services for electrical installations in both private and public projects, as well as professional electrical engineering services. We have extensive experience in the field of electromechanical studies and installations, operating as independent professionals since 1991. Our main areas of expertise include the design, supervision, and construction of electrical installations, the planning and implementation of indoor and outdoor lighting systems, lightning protection systems, building automation solutions, as well as the programming and installation of KNX systems.
